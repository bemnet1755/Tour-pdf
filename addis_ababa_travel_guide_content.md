## Quick Start: First 2 Hours After Landing

### ✈️ Step 1 — At Bole Airport Arrival Hall (0–20 min)
- Buy or activate local SIM/eSIM (for ride-hailing apps + maps).
- Exchange a small amount of cash for immediate expenses.
- Open transport apps: **RIDE**, **FERES**, **Yango**.

### 🚕 Step 2 — Get to Your Hotel Fast (20–60 min)
- Preferred: app-based ride for transparent pricing.
- Backup: airport taxi desk (ask fare estimate before departure).

### 🏨 Step 3 — Check-in and Re-orient (60–120 min)
- Confirm hotel Wi-Fi + breakfast hours.
- Save nearby essentials: pharmacy, ATM, coffee, convenience store.

## Section 1: Transportation

### 🚗 Top 5 Transportation Services in Addis Ababa

#### 1) RIDE (8294)
- **Short description:** Most widely used local ride-hailing platform; strong city coverage and call-center booking support.
- **Customer review summary:**
  - **Pros:** reliable dispatch, many drivers, app + short-code booking.
  - **Cons:** occasional driver calls for location clarification; surge pricing in peak traffic.
- **Minimum price per km:** **~15 ETB/km** (indicative; final fare depends on demand/time).
- **Availability:** **24/7** (best coverage daytime/evening).
- **Head office / support phone:** **8294** (dispatch/support short code).
- **App availability:** **iOS + Android**.

#### 2) FERES
- **Short description:** Ethiopia-born taxi-hailing app focused on safety and practical urban transport.
- **Customer review summary:**
  - **Pros:** good fare transparency, competitive pricing.
  - **Cons:** wait times can increase in rain/peak hours.
- **Minimum price per km:** **~13–15 ETB/km** (indicative).
- **Availability:** **24/7** in core zones; limited coverage on outer edges late night.
- **Head office phone:** **Public website contact available; often handled via in-app support + contact page.**
- **App availability:** **iOS + Android**.

#### 3) Yango
- **Short description:** International ride-hailing app operating in Addis with easy onboarding for travelers.
- **Customer review summary:**
  - **Pros:** user-friendly app, card/cash flexibility, promo campaigns.
  - **Cons:** driver supply can fluctuate by neighborhood/time.
- **Minimum price per km:** **~14–18 ETB/km** (indicative).
- **Availability:** **24/7** (strong in central business/leisure areas).
- **Head office / support phone:** **8610** (call-taxi service) + in-app support.
- **App availability:** **iOS + Android**.

#### 4) Airport Taxi (Bole official/queue taxis)
- **Short description:** On-site taxis outside arrivals; useful if your phone/data is unavailable.
- **Customer review summary:**
  - **Pros:** immediate access at terminal.
  - **Cons:** prices can be higher and less predictable than app rides if not negotiated.
- **Minimum price per km:** **~20+ ETB/km equivalent** (trip-based pricing common).
- **Availability:** **24/7** at airport.
- **Head office phone:** **Use airport taxi desk on arrival (operator varies).**
- **App availability:** **No dedicated app.**

#### 5) GuzoRide (private-hire/rental style)
- **Short description:** Driver-focused booking/rental option for longer city use and day plans.
- **Customer review summary:**
  - **Pros:** useful for multi-stop days and custom schedules.
  - **Cons:** fewer vehicles than major ride-hailing apps.
- **Minimum price per km:** **Custom quote / trip package**.
- **Availability:** **Extended hours; advance booking recommended.**
- **Head office phone:** **Website/in-app inquiry based.**
- **App availability:** **Web + mobile-friendly booking (check current app rollout).**

**Traveler Tip (highlight box):**  
📌 Always confirm pickup pin on the map and destination before starting ride. Keep small ETB notes for cash settlements.

## Section 2: Hotels

### 🏨 Top 5 Hotels in Addis Ababa

#### 1) Ethiopian Skylight Hotel
- **Rating:** 5-star (airport-adjacent flagship).
- **Average price/night:** **~20,000–35,000 ETB** (**~$128–$224**).
- **Key features:** airport shuttle, large conference facilities, multiple restaurants, gym, pool, Wi-Fi.
- **Location:** Bole area, very close to Addis Ababa Bole International Airport.
- **Ideal for:** business travelers, layovers, premium convenience.

#### 2) Hyatt Regency Addis Ababa
- **Rating:** 5-star international brand.
- **Average price/night:** **~28,000–45,000 ETB** (**~$180–$288**).
- **Key features:** high-speed Wi-Fi, wellness/spa, strong dining options, business-ready amenities.
- **Location:** Meskel Square / central diplomatic-business zone.
- **Ideal for:** executives, upscale leisure, first-time visitors wanting central access.

#### 3) Sheraton Addis, a Luxury Collection Hotel
- **Rating:** 5-star luxury.
- **Average price/night:** **~40,000–75,000 ETB** (**~$256–$480**).
- **Key features:** iconic grounds, luxury dining, pool, high-security setting, event venues.
- **Location:** near National Palace / city-center diplomatic corridor.
- **Ideal for:** luxury travelers, high-level business meetings, special occasions.

#### 4) Radisson Blu Hotel, Addis Ababa
- **Rating:** 5-star business-lifestyle.
- **Average price/night:** **~22,000–38,000 ETB** (**~$141–$244**).
